**HttpClient service in Angular has 9 methods** which are used to perform HTTP requests.

1. HttpClient.request() 
2. HttpClient.get() 
3. HttpClient.post() 
4. HttpClient.delete()
5. HttpClient.put() 
6. HttpClient.head()
7. HttpClient.jsonp()
8. HttpClient.options()
9. HttpClient.patch()

All the above methods returns an observable as response type.
